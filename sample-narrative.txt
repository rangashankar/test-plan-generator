PRESS RELEASE

Company Announces "Smart Assistant" - AI-Powered Digital Assistant That Learns User Preferences

Revolutionary new feature uses machine learning to predict what users need before they know it themselves

SEATTLE, WA - November 3, 2025 - Company today announced Smart Assistant, an innovative AI-powered digital assistant that transforms the user experience by learning user preferences and automatically suggesting actions, tracking tasks, and managing recurring activities. Smart Assistant represents our continued commitment to making digital interactions effortless and personalized for millions of users worldwide.

"Smart Assistant eliminates the friction from digital workflows," said [Product Leader]. "Our users told us they wanted an experience that understands their needs and helps them discover features they'll love while saving time and effort. Smart Assistant delivers exactly that."

Smart Assistant leverages advanced machine learning capabilities to analyze user behavior, activity history, and usage patterns to provide highly personalized recommendations. The feature can predict when users are likely to need certain actions and proactively suggest workflows, often before users realize they need them.

Key features of Smart Assistant include:

• Intelligent Task Discovery: Uses AI to surface relevant actions based on individual usage patterns and preferences
• Predictive Workflows: Automatically suggests when to perform routine tasks based on usage patterns
• Smart Notifications: Monitors user activity and provides timely alerts and reminders
• Voice Integration: Works seamlessly with voice commands for hands-free interactions
• Smart Suggestions: Recommends complementary actions that other users frequently perform together

The Smart Assistant feature will be available to all premium users starting December 2025, with plans to expand to all users in early 2026.

QUESTIONS AND ANSWERS

Q: How does Smart Assistant learn my preferences?
A: Smart Assistant analyzes your usage history, activity patterns, feature ratings, and interaction behavior to build a personalized profile. The more you use the system, the smarter it becomes. All data analysis happens securely within our systems and follows our strict privacy guidelines.

Q: Will Smart Assistant automatically perform actions without my permission?
A: No. Smart Assistant only makes suggestions and sends notifications. You maintain full control over all actions. For predictive workflows, you can set up automatic execution for specific tasks, but you'll always receive advance notice and can cancel or modify actions before they're processed.

Q: How accurate are Smart Assistant's predictions?
A: In our testing, Smart Assistant achieved 87% accuracy in predicting when users would need to perform routine tasks and 92% satisfaction rate for action recommendations. The system continuously learns and improves from user feedback.

Q: Can I turn off Smart Assistant features I don't want?
A: Absolutely. Smart Assistant is designed to be helpful, not intrusive. You can customize which features are active, adjust notification preferences, or disable Smart Assistant entirely through your account settings.

Q: How does Smart Assistant protect my privacy?
A: Smart Assistant uses the same robust security and privacy protections as all our services. Your usage data is encrypted, never shared with third parties for marketing purposes, and you can review and delete your data at any time. The AI models use aggregated, anonymized data to improve recommendations while protecting individual privacy.

Q: Will Smart Assistant work on mobile devices?
A: Yes, Smart Assistant is fully integrated into our mobile app for iOS and Android, as well as the desktop application. Features are optimized for each platform to provide the best user experience.

Q: How does Smart Assistant integrate with voice commands?
A: Smart Assistant works seamlessly with voice recognition systems. You can ask about your Smart Assistant suggestions, execute recommended actions using voice commands, and get notifications about task suggestions through voice interfaces.

Q: What if Smart Assistant suggests something I don't like?
A: You can easily provide feedback on any suggestion by rating it or marking it as "not interested." This feedback immediately improves future recommendations. Smart Assistant learns from both positive and negative feedback to better understand your preferences.

Q: Will Smart Assistant increase my workload?
A: Smart Assistant is designed to help you find actions you actually need and want to perform, often at better times through smart scheduling. Many beta users reported saving time by catching important tasks they might have forgotten and avoiding unnecessary actions they didn't really need to perform.
